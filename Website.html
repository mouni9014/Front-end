About Tom and Jerry

Tom and Jerry is an iconic American animated series created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera. The show features the comedic rivalry between Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse, known for its slapstick humor and clever antics.

Main Characters

Tom

Tom is the determined yet often outsmarted cat who constantly tries to catch Jerry. His elaborate plans are usually foiled by Jerry's quick wits.

Jerry

Jerry is a clever mouse who lives in the same house as Tom. He often uses his intelligence and agility to stay one step ahead of Tom.

Popular Episodes

  • The Cat Concerto - Tom's piano performance is interrupted by Jerry's antics.
  • Mouse Trouble - Tom uses various traps to catch Jerry, with comical results.
  • Tom and Jerry in the Hollywood Bowl - A musical episode featuring their escapades in a concert hall.
